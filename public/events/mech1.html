Maaticha Khel

Team Size:

🔹Individual or a team of maximum 2 participants.

Model Requirements

🔹The models should be at a 1:24 scale.
🔹The top 3 models will be selected based on their closeness to the reference model chosen by participants, considering aspects such as body lines, wheels, headlights, and other details.

Round 1: Automobile Quiz

Format:
🔹10 questions (1 mark each) – No negative marking.
🔹15 questions (2 marks each) – Negative marking applies.
🔹Cover Letter Writing (10 marks) – Participants must draft a cover letter as if they are applying to an automotive company.

Rules

🔹Strict invigilation; use of mobile devices is prohibited.
🔹Time Limit: 45 minutes.

Round 2: Clay Modelling

Part 1: Research

🔹Participants are allowed to use the internet to research car models, procedures, and tutorial videos.
🔹They can watch YouTube videos and replicate techniques, but the final selection is based on the quality of their model.
🔹Participants must memorize their model and modeling is to be done with no reference.
🔹During the modeling, participants will have one chance to view their reference model for 2 minutes only.
🔹No prior preparation is needed; participants will have dedicated time to research and develop techniques.
🔹Time Limit: 30 minutes.

Part 2: Modelling

🔹After research, participants must turn off their devices and start working on their clay models.
🔹Time Limit: 1 hour 30 minutes.

Judging Criteria

🔹Top 3 models will be selected based on their closeness to the real product and the quality of craftsmanship.

View Rules & Regulations

🔹Participants must arrive on time for each round.
🔹The use of external USB drives or pre-saved files is not allowed.
🔹The judges' decision is final; no re-evaluation requests will be entertained.
🔹Any form of misconduct or cheating will result in immediate disqualification.
