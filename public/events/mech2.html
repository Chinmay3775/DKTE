CAD War 2k25

Round 1: AutoCAD Quiz (Elimination Round)

Format:

🔹 15 multiple-choice and short-answer questions.
🔹 Time Limit: 20–30 minutes.

Scoring

🔹 Each correct answer = 1 point.
🔹 No negative marking.

Rules:

🔹 No use of AutoCAD software, books, or internet resources.
🔹 Answers must be submitted within the time limit.

Round 2: 2D Drafting Challenge

Task:
🔹 Participants must create a detailed 2D drawing based on a given technical problem.
🔹 Time Limit: 60 minutes.

Rules:

🔹 Participants must complete the task using AutoCAD only.
🔹 Use of pre-made blocks is not allowed (unless specified).
🔹 Internet search is not allowed.

Round 3: Advanced 2D/3D Design (Final Round)

Task:
🔹 Participants must create either a complex 2D drawing or a 3D model based on a real-world engineering problem.
🔹 Time Limit: 90 minutes.

Rules:

🔹 The challenge will be revealed at the beginning of the round.
🔹 Participants must save and submit their files in .DWG format.
🔹 Any attempt to copy from others will lead to disqualification.

View Rules & Regulations

🔹 Participants must arrive on time for each round.
🔹 The use of external USB drives or pre-saved files is not allowed.
🔹 The judges' decision is final; no re-evaluation requests will be entertained.
🔹 Any form of misconduct or cheating will result in immediate disqualification.
