Tech Symposium 2K25

The Tech Nova 2.0

Rules:

  • Maximum two participants per team.
  • Any coding language can be used.
  • Novice group – F.Y. and S.Y.
  • Expert group – T.Y. and Final year.

Leaderboard:

• There will be a live leaderboard showing scores of each team.
• Teams will have ranks.
• The leaderboard will be displayed after each round.

Round 1: “Logic Leagues”

Format: Aptitude test followed by a rapid-fire bonus round with logic puzzles.
Additional challenges: Introduce a "Double or Nothing" question for bonus points.

Participants: All registered teams.

Scoring: Based on performance and marks.

Selection Criteria: Top teams based on aptitude and rapid round performance will advance.

Time Limit: Specified time will be given for each phase.

Round 2: “Code Cleanse Challenge”

Format: Debugging round with a streak bonus and "Code Chaos" twist for hints.
Teams can earn a "Swap Bug" option once per round.

Participants: Teams shortlisted from Round 1.

Task: Fix bugs in code snippets or programs within the time limit.

Scoring: Based on the number of bugs fixed and streak bonuses.

Selection Criteria: Teams meeting required criteria will qualify for the final round.

Round 3: “Pro Solver's Paradise”

Format: A coding contest to solve problems within a time limit.
Additional challenges: "Double Points Mode" for selected challenges, with penalties for failure.

Scoring: Based on questions solved and solution optimization.

Selection Criteria: The team with the highest rank on the leaderboard will win.
